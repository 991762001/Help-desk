Reason for the Plan

Improve internal Help Desk operations for the upcoming fiscal year.

Mission

Ensure efficient IT support, enhancing user satisfaction and productivity.

Vision

Be the top Help Desk for reliable, quick issue resolution.

Goals

Goal 1: Reduce average time to respond by 10% by year end.

Benefit: Faster resolution improves employee workflow.

Goal 2: Increase first contact resolution rate by 10% by year end.

Benefit: Minimize repeated support requests.

Goal 3: Decrease incident resolution time by 10% by year end.

Benefit: Improve operational efficiency.

Goal 4: Increase self-service effectiveness by 10% by year end.

Benefit: Reduce dependency on live support.

Goal 5: Decrease backlog by 15% by year end.

Benefit: Faster ticket processing and resolution.
